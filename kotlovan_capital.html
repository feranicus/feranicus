How the safe purchase actually works

1
Use an independent Georgian lawyer — not the developer's

Title-history & encumbrance check via the House of Justice, contract drafting, document translation. Registration fees are tiny (GEL 50–200 at NAPR; notary up to GEL 200).

2
Register ownership at NAPR (National Agency of Public Registry)

The notarized deed is filed electronically and title is registered — usually within a day. A buyer needn't even be present; an authorised representative can file with a notarised power of attorney.

3
Stage payments to construction milestones

Tie installments to delivered stages (signing → frame → roof → keys). For extra safety, route funds through a bank-held / escrow arrangement (Bank of Georgia, TBC) rather than direct developer transfers.

4
Vet the developer like an underwriter

Past delivered projects, financial standing, permits and zoning. Foreigners can fully own non-agricultural property — no residence permit needed to buy, and no purchase tax or stamp duty.

5
Residency, if the investor wants it

A property-based residence permit is available — but the threshold rose to $150,000 from 1 March 2026 (was $100,000). Position it as an upside, never a guarantee.

The money-movement reality (handle it openly)

¥
The $50,000 SAFE limit

Mainland individuals can freely convert/remit only ~US$50,000 per person per year, and FX rules formally bar using that quota for overseas housing. Above it needs SAFE approval. This is the #1 practical constraint — design around it.

✓
Legitimate routes investors use

Hong Kong / Singapore bank accounts and existing offshore funds, family pooling across several relatives' quotas, and capital already held abroad. Pair the buyer with cross-border tax/legal advisers — never coach anyone to break currency law.

!
Tighter KYC from 2026

From 1 Jan 2026, stricter verification applies to outbound transfers over ~RMB 5,000 / US$1,000. Build compliance and proof-of-funds into the funnel from the first message.

★
HK & Singapore are the easier audiences

No mainland FX cap — they can move capital freely. Often the fastest closers, and the right place to start the campaign while the mainland funnel matures.
