Memorandum of Understanding

Between CyberGod LLC and Arturo Blanco

Purpose

The purpose of this MOU is to establish the preliminary agreement between CyberGod LLC and Arturo Blanco regarding his role as a core team member of the company, his equity stake, and the mutual goals and vision for the company's growth.

1. Overview of CyberGod LLC

CyberGod LLC aims to revolutionize the cybersecurity landscape by providing next-generation Endpoint Protection Platform (EPP) solutions. These solutions will adhere to European regulations:

  • DORA (Digital Operational Resilience Act): Ensuring operational resilience against IT disruptions and cyber threats.
  • NIS2 (Network and Information Systems Directive): Enhancing the security of critical infrastructure across the EU.
  • CRA (Cyber Resilience Act): Securing digital products and services with continuous monitoring and risk mitigation.

2. Arturo Blanco’s Role and Contribution

As a core team member, Arturo will:

  • Lead information systems and technology initiatives to ensure a robust and scalable architecture.
  • Develop and oversee cybersecurity strategies aligned with industry best practices and emerging threats.
  • Provide part-time (20-30 hours/week) remote work, with the flexibility to accommodate his schedule.

3. Equity Stake

Arturo will be allocated equity in CyberGod LLC based on his contribution as one of the founding core team members. The exact percentage will be determined during the incorporation process.

4. Work Environment

  • Remote-First: All team members can work from any location.
  • Open-Door Policy: Ensures unrestricted communication between team members.

5. Future Prospects

CyberGod LLC envisions creating a transformative cybersecurity platform that will:

  • Reduce dependency on traditional EPP solutions with scalable and compliance-friendly alternatives.
  • Pioneer advancements in AI-driven threat detection and mitigation.
  • Expand across industries, including critical infrastructure and SMBs.

6. Next Steps

  • Finalize Equity Terms: Equity allocation details to be formalized post-incorporation.
  • Discuss Project Details: Schedule a meeting to finalize Arturo’s role and CyberGod’s roadmap.

By signing below, both parties agree to the terms outlined in this MOU and express their intent to collaborate towards CyberGod LLC's success.
