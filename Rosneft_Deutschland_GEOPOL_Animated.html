Scene 04 · Featured issue — Check Point VPN takedown · SASE/ZTNA is the quick win

Six moves arrive. Each layer stops its own.

The headline finding in motion: Rosneft DE's Check Point Remote-Access gateway (217.110.113.116) is public and carries CVE-2026-50751 (CISA KEV · CVSS 9.3). A Qilin ransomware affiliate opens with a volumetric DDoS to flood the edge and mask the intrusion, bypasses the IKEv1 gateway login, pivots to ERP and refinery OT, deploys ransomware and exfiltrates data. Colt IP Guardian scrubs the flood at the network edge and SASE / ZTNA retires the public gateway so there is nothing left to exploit — both without a rip-and-replace.
