/ Safe by design

Autonomy with the brakes on

Human owns production

Nothing live without a “yes”

Routine intel→sandbox cycles are autonomous, but no AI-authored add-on reaches production without an authorised engineer's sign-off and a maintenance window.

Awareness, not weapons

No live malware, ever

Dark-net intel informs safe simulations only. The forge generates fingerprint-and-validate probes — it never downloads, stores or runs live malware against production.

🎯

Hard scope

Models can only target in-scope subnets; SaaS & partner nets are blocked at the orchestration layer.

🧪

Sandbox-first

Every generated add-on passes lab safety checks before it can target anything.

🛑

Kill switch

“Kill All Agents” stays available; every decision & add-on is logged for audit.

🔐

Secrets isolated

Credentials sit apart from the models, in a separate container.
