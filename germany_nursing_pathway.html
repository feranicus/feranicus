/ Read this first

One rule protects everything

Ethiopia is on the WHO list of countries whose health systems must be protected, so agencies and hospitals are not allowed to actively recruit nurses there. That sounds like a wall — but it is not. It only stops them from chasing you. It never stops you from applying yourself.

The thing to remember

You may apply directly, on your own. And you never pay to get a job.

Because you reach out first — through a hospital's careers page or an official German channel — a German employer is fully allowed to hire you. The recruitment fee, the language course, the relocation: a fair employer covers these. If anyone asks you for money to "guarantee" a job, that is the scam, not the system.

/ Protect yourself

Spot the scam in seconds

Demand for nurses brings people who prey on hope. Every sign below means walk away. A real, ethical offer breaks none of these.

✕
Upfront fees. Anyone asking you to pay for a "guaranteed job", a slot, or processing is a scam. You do not pay to be hired.
✕
"We can bypass the WHO list." Nobody can, and nobody needs to — you apply directly, legally, for free.
✕
Vague contract. No named employer, no city, no salary, no working hours? Do not sign.
✕
Pressure to decide today. Real processes take months. Urgency is a manipulation tactic.
✕
Pay-it-back penalties. A fair repayment clause is small and transparent; a "penalty" that traps you is not.
✕
Only chat apps, no official email. A real employer or agency has a verifiable company, address, and seal.
✓

The safe sign: the employer covers recruitment costs, names itself clearly, and — if an agency is involved — holds the German "Fair Recruitment Care Germany" (Faire Anwerbung Pflege) quality seal.
